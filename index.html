Muktinath Tours

Our Muktinath Temple Tour is one of the famous tours in Nepal Tour Package & considered a Pilgrimage tour in Nepal,

which combines a natural glimpse of Pokhara along with Muktinath Temple. Muktinath Temple is also well known for Muktidham, as Hindus religion believes as Mukti Kshetra which means "place of liberation or moksha". Muktinath Temple has located 48 km northeast of Jomsom an elevation of 3710 m. Muktinath Temple is for Lord Narayana with its pagoda-shaped temple, which is dedicated to Lord Vishnu. Also, there are 108 waterspouts cast behind the main temple and it is the shape of cow-headed. Lots of prayers take bath with the ice-cold water. During the Hindu festival at Janai Purnima, most of the devotees gather here to pay worship to Muktinath Temple.

Kailash Mansarovar Yatra

Kailash overland tour from Kerung border underway as an alternative route, which is easily accessible to Mount Kailash from the suburbs of Nepal

This tour starts with sightseeing in Kathmandu. As an alternative, you can also make a visit to Muktinath by arriving in Nepal a few days earlier. Soon after this, we will have an enchanting view of the Langtang National Park and cross the Nepal- Tibet border to arrive at Kerung. Reaching there, we will drive to the villages of Saga, Mansarovar, and Darchen with great views of the sacred Mt. Kailash, Mt. Gurla-Mandala, Rakshash Lake, and Lake Mansarovar. As per the itinerary, on day 8, we will initiate the 3 day-long Kailash Kora. The objective of this tour is to view the gigantic landscape of the sacred top of Kailash, Holy Lake of Mansarovar, and the highest pass of Drolma La. As the journey of the Kailash Overland Tour takes; we can curtail or extend our trip as per our requirements. The most suitable time to visit Mt. Kailash – is between May to August.

Bhutan Tours

"Best of Bhutan Tour" is perfectly designed to give a unique and incredible vacation experience to travelers who wish to mingle with the locals,

"Best of Bhutan Tour" is perfectly designed to give a unique and incredible vacation experience to travelers who wish to mingle with the locals, dip in a traditional wooden tub of hot stones and visit awesome places and enjoy the intricate art and architecture which is evident in almost ubiquitously. This tour package in Bhutan is a popular trip as it offers a number of days to delve deeper into the countryside that offers invaluable insight into the rich culture and traditions of the tiny nation. Besides, this is an easy tour in Bhutan which is accompanied by breathtaking panoramic views of snow-capped peaks of the Himalayas from various high mountain passes. This cultural tour in Bhutan revolves around the historical and cultural highlights of the towns such as Paro, Thimphu, Punakha, Trongsa, Bumthang, and Gangtey.
